CRAVE TECH stands as a premier digital solutions provider in Bacolod City, trusted by businesses, schools, and government units across the Negros Island Region since 2012. For more than a decade, we have been committed to transforming ideas into impactful digital experiences through cutting-edge technology and innovative services. What began as a company focused on LED advertising—delivering LED billboards, large-format displays, digital signages, and CCTVs across Negros Occidental—has grown into a comprehensive hub for digital solutions. Over the years, our expertise has expanded to include software development, allowing us to create customized, efficient, and reliable systems that empower organizations to thrive in an increasingly digital world. At CRAVE TECH, our mission is simple yet powerful: to bridge technology and creativity in order to meet the evolving needs of our clients and to drive progress throughout the Negros Island Region and beyond.

Our Mission

Crave's single mission is to make our client's businesses stronger by continuously providing the latest and most innovative digital business solutions available.

Our Vision

To be the leader in digital business solutions in Bacolod City and the province of Negros Occidental through creative marketing, exceeding customer expectations and sustainable growth.
